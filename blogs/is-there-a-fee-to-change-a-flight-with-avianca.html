Is there a fee to change a flight with Avianca?

For the feasibility of the passengers of Avianca, so they never face any trouble while utilizing different services of Avianca, it has provided a very low fee to change your Avianca flight; please give a look at the mentioned points, which will assist you in knowing the fares:

  • The Avianca flight change fee is USD 25 to USD 150. (it may vary from time to time, for better clearance, you can reach out to the customer executive by calling 1(800) 284-2622.

  • The fares that are charged may vary from destination to destination and also from a different class to class.

Policy, Process, and fare to change flights with Avianca 

Avianca Airlines provides different facilities to passengers, for example, an extra-baggage policy, easy booking and canceling policy, lounge facilities, a pet policy, and various other policies and services. If you have already booked your tickets with Avianca and you want to change your flight but do not know how to get in touch with Avianca Airlines customer Support Department, what is the procedure for changing the flight, and what are the fees? Please have a look at the mentioned details:

Understand the policy of Avianca Flight change:

Before knowing about the fares and the procedure of flight change with Avianca, one must understand the Avianca change flight policy, which will provide you with a brief picture of the process of changing a flight:

  • You can change your flight 24 hours prior to your scheduled flight with Avianca Airlines. But if you do it, you may have to incur additional costs to change your Avianca flight.

  • To change a flight, make sure you have booked your reservation 7 days in advance.

  • Within 24 hours of your booking, you are flexible to change your flight at no additional cost.

  • You may have to incur a difference in fare if the date or the route you choose has a higher fare. 

  • If any ticket is purchased with LifeMiles, you will not be able to change your flights.

How do I change my Avianca flight?

If you have decided to change your flight with Avianca Airlines due to any reason, for example, Someone has fallen ill, poor weather, etc. There are very flexible ways by which you can easily change your Avianca flight, and you will never raise a question of how I can change my Avianca flight. Look at the mentioned ways:

Change your flights on Avianca Website.

You can change your flight with Avianca, as it provides a very flexible opportunity to passengers, so they never experience any issues while availing of different services. So, below are the steps:

  • Visit the official website of Avianca Airlines. (www.avianca.com)

  • Then under the “Manage my bookings” section, you are required to tap on “Flight Status.”

  • Then choose a flight which you wish to change. (Date, timings, destination, etc.)

  • Once done, now you can choose your flight.

  • Then, review all the Terms and conditions. (you may have to incur additional pay if there is a difference in fares.

  • Then, you are supposed to complete the process by clicking on “Send Receipt.”

Change your flight on Call.

You can also change your Avianca flight a call by calling on Avianca change flight Phone number +1 800-722-8222, and once you dial a number, you will listen to certain IVR options that you have to choose from on the basis of your required service:

  • Press 1 and choose your language.

  • Press 2 to change your flight.

  • Press 3 to book your ticket

  • Press 4 for other options.

How many times can I change my flight Avianca?

To change your flight with Avianca, there are different norms or times at which you can change your flight, have a look at the below points:

  • If you are flying on the routes operated by Avianca, except for some domestic routers, you can change only 1 time. 

  • On some specific routes, it allows passengers to change flights 3 times. (it depends on the destination and routes.)
