How Can I Speak to Someone on Hawaiian Airlines?

Often while traveling with Hawaiian Airlines, passengers complain about various issues they face during their journey & want instant solutions to resolve the inconvenience caused. Also, sometimes people wish to cancel their reservations at the last moment due to a sudden change of plan & want to talk to someone at Hawaiian Airlines?. In the forthcoming section, the alternatives & procedures of the same are described in detail.

Different ways to reach the Hawaiian Airlines Customer Services

If any customer faces any issue during their journey via Hawaiian airlines, then they can simply share their feedback by option any one of the below-mentioned options:

  • Via phone number: 1-800-367-5320
  • By using the live chat option
  • By sending an email
  • Writing a mail

It is completely up to the customers which option they choose to proceed with. In the forthcoming section, a detailed description is given of each of the above alternatives. You can refer to the information clearly and use your method to Speak to Hawaiian Airlines Customer Service accordingly.

connect with Hawaiian Airlines Via phone number:

This is one of the most preferred mediums to contact Hawaiian airlines. People like to express their issues personally; hence they opt for it. Though there are often chances when the customers complain about busy channels, still it is preferred as it lets you talk to a live person at Hawaiian Ailrines & avail solutions. You need to dial: 1-800-367-5320, which is available 24/7 at Hawaiian Airlines, and select one of the options from the following IVR menu to coonect with live representative:

  • Press 1: To choose your language
  • Press 2: To make a reservation
  • Press 3: To manage booking
  • Press 4: To request a special assistance
  • Press 9: To request a callback

After pressing one of the options, your call will be connected to the corresponding representative who will help you to resolve your problem.

By using a live chat option:

Another alternative to reach the airline & get rid of your travel issue is by chatting with the customer team. Many times people are confused about whether they can  Speak to live customer service by using this option or not. The answer is very simple; yes, customers can request a callback at their desired date & time using the live chat option. To avail of this option, the following instructions must be obeyed:

  • Visit the official Hawaiian airline website & from the top menu bar, select the option of “contact us.”
  • When you scroll down on the page, you will come across a chat icon, tap on it & type your message in the chat box.
  • Finally, hit the arrow icon to send your message.
  • Shortly you can talk to someone at Hawaiian Airlines and a representative will assist you who will generate the finest possible solution to your issue.

By sending an email:

If you are more comfortable expressing your issue by writing, you can surely use this method. Hawaiian airlines let you draft & share your feedback or concern in the format of sending a complaint form. To use it, customers are advised to follow the given instructions carefully;

  • Visit the website & navigate to the contact us section & proceed to click on “email us.”
  • Now you will discover a form in which you must fill out all the mandatory information.
  • You must write your issue in the message box & attach any relevant document in the prescribed format.
  • Finally, press the submit button & you will be reverted as soon as your message gets acknowledged.

Writing a mail:

The customers can also write their issue & send it to the Hawaiian airlines mailing address: 

Hawaiian Airlines, P.O. Box 30008, Honolulu, HI 96820

Though it might take some time to receive a revert, it is still a good way to contact the airline & expect their response.
